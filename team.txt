DELETE Branches in VS Code:
LOCAL:
    - open "Command Palette" (Ctrl+Shift+P on Windows/Linux, CMD+Shift+P on Mac)
    - Select "Git: Delete Branch..."
    - Choose branch to delete

REMOTE:
    - open "Command Palette" (Ctrl+Shift+P on Windows/Linux, CMD+Shift+P on Mac)
    - Select "Git: Fetch (Prune)"
        OR
    - open "Terminal"
    - enter "git fetch --prune"
